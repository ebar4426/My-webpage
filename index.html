The Crew

Michael De Santa

The leader of the operation, very head strong and impulsive. Michael makes major decisions for their three man crew threw out the story for heists and big missions. Has a history with Trevor Phillips and meets up with Franklin Clinton early on in the game. Michael has a Wife named Amanda, a Son named Jimmy and a Daughter named Tracey. The main reason he left his life of crime was to get closer to his kids but over the years this hasen't been the case he is getting farther and farther away from them. He meets Franklin by chance, Franklin Clinton tried to steal Michael's Sons SUV that was over priced, the then go to the dealer ship and fix what was going on. Michael then meets up with Franklin and decides to take him under his wing and show him how to be a true criminal. during the process they are forced to preform a heist that creates enough publicity that Trevor immediately identifies Michael and decides to pay his old presumed dead best friend a visit. Special Ability: Slow time on foot during fire fight's.

Trevor Phillips

The CRAZY best friend of Michael De Santa, and new friend of Franklin Clinton. Trevor is the muscle in the crew, has psychopathic tendencies and issues with anger. very unpredictable but very good with weapons and flying almost anything. Trevor gets the job done what ever the cost. Ever since the heist that went south, Trevor thought Michael had died and morned the loss of his good friend, over the years he started a criminal organization that he calls Trevor Phillips inc. He cooks drugs and sells guns around the world he then sees a TV news report about a heist where one of the suspects uses a cheesy line from a famous movie, this immediately puts trevor on edge because he knows that Michael is still alive and has deceived him and has been alive all these years. He then decides to go looking for him in the city of Los Santos and catch up with his old best friend. Special Ability: Strength rage, deal more damage to enemies and take less damage from them.

Franklin Clinton

The new guy to the team. Franklin is very calm and collected, he thinks about the situation and then acts. Franklin is usually the versatile one, he can be the getaway driver along with being in the actual fight. Franklin meets Michael De Santa on a presumed repo. job of a SUV as he is driving off Michael comes out of the back seat and tells him to take him to his boss. Michael then tells Franklin to drive the SUV thew the glass of the ripoff car lot. Franklin does as he is told and is invited for drinks later on with Michael. When Franklin goes by Michaels son Jimmy is in big trouble they both go and save him. Michael then decides to take him under his wing and show him how to become a true criminal instead of a two bit gangbanger. They are then forced to preform a heist which draws the attention of Trevor Phillips who then decides to pay them a visit. Special Ability: Slowdown time wile driving, increased handeling and traction.

These three Criminals all find each other by chance and become a real force to be reckoned with.
